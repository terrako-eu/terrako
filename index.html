TERRAKO: KEY MILESTONES

Highlighting the key milestones that have shaped Terrako's journey. Continually improving, we strive to provide the best possible user experience
with each new development with all stakeholders (Terrako Team, Sales companies, Dealers, Project Partners).

These milestones represent our ongoing efforts to innovate and serve our dealers and customers better.

Timeline with Centered Circles and Hover Effect

Milestone 1: Project initiation and creation

From 2019 to 2023


Kubota Group initiated the project to create an online marketplace, collaborating with internal teams and external experts to design and build the platform according to business needs.

Milestone 2: B2B Pilot Phase

From 2023 to 2024


Terrako Parts Ordering, began in September 2023 with the pilot launch in the UK, followed a few weeks later by the French pilot launch.

Milestone 3: B2B Full Launch

From 2024


Ordering via Terrako has been extended to the entire network in the UK in January, and gradually since May in France.

Milestone 4: B2C E-Commerce

End of 2024


The platform has extended to B2C to include end-customers starting with the pilot launch end of 2024.
