TERRAKO: KEY MILESTONES

Highlighting the key milestones that have shaped Terrako's journey. Continually improving, we strive to provide the best possible user experience
with each new development with all stakeholders (Terrako Team, Sales companies, Dealers, Project Partners).

Those milestones represent our ongoing efforts to innovate and serve our dealers and customers better.

Timeline with Centered Circles and Hover Effect

Milestone 1 & 2: Project initiation and creation

From 2019 to 2023


Kubota Group initiated the project to create an online marketplace, collaborating with internal teams and external experts to design and build the platform according to business needs.

Milestone 3: B2B Pilot Phase

From 2023 to 2024


Terrako Parts Ordering, began in September 2023 with the launch for 6 pilot dealers in UK, followed a few weeks later by 6 French pilot dealers.

Milestone 4: B2B Full Launch

From 2024


Ordering via Terrako has been extended to the entire network in UK in January, and gradually since May in France.

Milestone 5: B2C E-Commerce

From 2025


The platform has been in real-world testing with a UK pilot dealer since September, with ongoing improvements and new features being added.

TERRAKO: TOWARDS THE FUTURE

B2B: Expansion
Over the next three years, we will expand
into new countries, bringing Terrako’s
solutions to more regions and industries.

B2C: E-Commerce:
We will roll out the e-commerce platform
country by country in Europe, adding new features to enhance the user experience
for both dealers and customers.

B2C: Marketplace:
In 2025, we will launch
the marketplace. Initially, we will integrate
existing partners and then focus on expanding by onboarding new partners to grow the business.
